Cockroach Problems And Preventive Measures: To keep away from cockroach problems, follow these preventive measures: 1. Maintain cleanliness: Keep your home or workplace clean and tidy. Regularly sweep, vacuum, mop floors, and wipe down surfaces to eliminate food particles and potential hiding spots for cockroaches. 2. Dispose of garbage properly: Ensure that trash is placed in sealed bins and taken out regularly. Avoid leaving garbage bags inside your living or working spaces for extended periods. 3. Seal cracks and crevices: Inspect your property for cracks, gaps, or openings in walls, floors, and windows. Seal them with caulk or putty to prevent cockroaches from entering. 4. Fix leaks: Cockroaches need water to survive, so repair any plumbing leaks promptly. Also, ensure that no standing water accumulates in sinks or other areas. 5. Use screens: Install screens on windows and doors to prevent cockroaches from entering your home or workplace. 6.
